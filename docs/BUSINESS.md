# Vocare Courses — Business Context

## What Vocare Is

Vocare is a Danish company that provides B2B phone sales services (mødebooking / meeting booking). The Vocare Courses platform is a training tool for people learning to sell over the phone — specifically cold calling and B2B outbound sales in the Danish market.

## The Course Philosophy

The course is written by Christopher, who has 12 years of B2B/B2C sales experience. His core philosophy:

- **Authenticity over technique** — how you say it matters more than what you say. If you don't believe in the product, no technique will save you.
- **Practical over strategic** — call 100 prospects instead of spending months on market analysis. Learn by doing.
- **Humble but persistent** — the best openers are simple and respectful, not clever tricks.
- **"Fake it till you make it"** — on bad days, act confident until it becomes real again.

## Target Audience

- Danish-speaking sales professionals (new and experienced)
- B2B phone sales reps learning cold calling
- People working in mødebooking (meeting booking) agencies
- Anyone wanting to improve their outbound sales skills

## Course Structure (8 Chapters)

1. **Introduktion** — 12 years of experience distilled. Why authenticity beats technique.
2. **Fundamentet** — Why 70% quit after 3 months. The value of pressure and failure.
3. **ICP + Skarpe Budskaber** — Finding your ideal customer profile. Benefit-focused messaging with open questions.
4. **De Første 10 Sekunder** — Opening calls with humility. Handling gatekeepers. LinkedIn research.
5. **Behovsafdækning** — Using open-ended questions. Active listening. Finding pain points. Using their own words.
6. **Pitch og Værdiskabelse** — EFU framework (Egenskaber → Fordele → Udbytte). Social proof.
7. **Indvendingsbehandling** — Three-part response: "Ja, det forstår jeg... men..." Handling time, budget, disinterest.
8. **Closing + Pipeline** — A/B closing (alternative close). Assumptive closing. Follow-up tactics. "Har vi opgivet dette?"

## Key Concepts Referenced in the Course

- **ICP** — Ideal Customer Profile. The 20-30% best customers, not the average.
- **EFU** — Egenskaber (Features), Fordele (Benefits), Udbytte (Outcomes). Danish sales framework.
- **Kunstpauser** — Strategic pauses that force responses and create authority.
- **A/B-lukket** — Alternative close: choice between two yeses, never yes/no.
- **Social Proof** — B2B buyers don't want to be guinea pigs. Reference similar companies.
- **"Har vi opgivet dette?"** — Christopher's follow-up tactic for dead conversations. Works on ego-driven decision makers.

## Business Model

- Payment is handled completely outside the platform (bank transfer, invoice, etc.)
- Admin generates access tokens (VOCARE-XXXX-XXXX) and gives them to paying customers
- No subscription management, no payment processing, no Stripe
- One token = one customer = full course access
